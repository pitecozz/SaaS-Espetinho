# **App Name**: Espetinho Vision

## Core Features:

- Instant Sale Logging: Record sales rapidly using photo cards and large icons, minimizing touches.
- Real-Time Profit Display: Display the day's net profit prominently on the home screen.
- Automated Audit Logs: Automatically log the creation, modification, and deletion of sales, pricing adjustments, and expenses to provide total traceability.
- Withdrawal Tracking: Require registration of personal withdrawals and display the impact on monthly profits.
- Loss Control Registration: Record surpluses and inventory breakages, with automatic adjustments to the Cost of Goods Sold (COGS).
- Performance Dashboard: Use bar graphs to present an immediate visual alert for operations running at a loss.
- AI-Powered Financial Tool: Suggest adjustments to spending to achieve financial goals

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant yellow (#FFC107) to evoke energy and optimism in financial management.
- Background color: Light yellow (#FAF9F0) to create a warm and inviting user interface.
- Accent color: Orange (#FF9800) for interactive elements and crucial alerts, highlighting key actions.
- Font pairing: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for headlines to maintain a contemporary and clean aesthetic, paired with 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for body text to ensure readability and accessibility.
- Use distinct icons for sales, expenses, and withdrawals.  Incorporate color-coded icons to quickly distinguish profit (green), expenses (red), and neutral actions (gray).
- Design buttons and navigation for one-handed use. Prioritize key functions on the main screen for rapid access, especially sales and profit views.
- Introduce subtle transitions during data loading to enhance perceived performance. Implement progress animations when calculating profits or generating reports to keep users informed.
